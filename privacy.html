PRIVACY

LAST UPDATED: June 15, 2025

METAJOURNAL IS DESIGNED WITH PRIVACY AS A CORE PRINCIPLE. ALL MEDIA PROCESSING, TRANSCRIPTION, AND ANALYSIS HAPPENS ENTIRELY ON YOUR DEVICE. YOUR PHOTOS, VIDEOS, AND TRANSCRIPTS NEVER LEAVE YOUR PHONE.

DATA PROCESSED LOCALLY

  • PHOTOS AND VIDEOS FROM YOUR SELECTED ALBUM
  • AUDIO EXTRACTED FROM VIDEOS FOR TRANSCRIPTION
  • TRANSCRIPTS GENERATED FROM VIDEO AUDIO
  • APP SETTINGS AND PREFERENCES
  • PROCESSING JOB STATUS AND METADATA

DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

  • YOUR PHOTOS OR VIDEOS ARE NEVER UPLOADED
  • TRANSCRIPTS REMAIN ON YOUR DEVICE
  • NO ACCESS BEYOND YOUR SPECIFIED ALBUM(S)
  • NO PERSONAL INFO TRANSMITTED TO EXTERNAL SERVICES

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

ALL DATA PROCESSING OCCURS LOCALLY FOR:

  • MONITORING YOUR SELECTED PHOTOS ALBUM
  • EXTRACTING AUDIO FROM VIDEOS
  • GENERATING SEARCHABLE TRANSCRIPTS
  • CREATING TIMELINE VIEWS
  • ENABLING SEARCH FUNCTIONALITY

DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

LOCAL STORAGE:

  • ALL DATA STORED USING APPLE'S SWIFTDATA
  • AUDIO FILES IN APP'S PRIVATE DIRECTORY
  • ENCRYPTED TRANSCRIPT STORAGE
  • PROTECTED BY IOS SECURITY FEATURES

ICLOUD SYNC:

  • WILL BE USING APPLES NATIVE SERVICES WITH PLATFORM PRIVACY CONCEPTS
  • IF YOU CHOOSE TO DELETE FROM CAMERA ROLL AND MOVE TO ICLOUD DRIVE FILES USING OUR APP WE DON'T TAKE ANY LIABILITY FOR LOSS OF DATA

PERMISSIONS REQUIRED

PHOTO LIBRARY ACCESS:

  • MONITOR SELECTED ALBUM FOR NEW MEDIA
  • ACCESS ONLY CHOSEN ALBUM CONTENT
  • GENERATE THUMBNAILS AND PROCESS VIDEOS

SPEECH RECOGNITION:

  • ON-DEVICE TRANSCRIPTION OF VIDEO AUDIO
  • USES APPLE'S LOCAL FRAMEWORKS
  • NO EXTERNAL SPEECH SERVICES

CAMERA AND AUDIO ACCESS:

  • ACCESS TO CAMERA AND AUDIO FOR JOURNAL RECORDING

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

METAJOURNAL OPERATES WITHOUT THIRD-PARTY SERVICES:

  • NO ANALYTICS OR TRACKING SERVICES
  • NO EXTERNAL APIS
  • NO ADVERTISING NETWORKS
  • ONLY APPLE'S NATIVE FRAMEWORKS

DATA RETENTION

  • DATA REMAINS ON YOUR DEVICE UNTIL DELETED
  • DELETING APP REMOVES ALL DATA
  • INDIVIDUAL ENTRIES CAN BE DELETED
  • AUDIO FILES AUTOMATICALLY MANAGED

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

METAJOURNAL DOES NOT COLLECT PERSONAL INFORMATION FROM CHILDREN UNDER 13. ALL PROCESSING IS LOCAL AND NO PERSONAL INFORMATION IS TRANSMITTED.
